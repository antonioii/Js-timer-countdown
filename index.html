Countdown Timer

: :
  1. Enter a time in the format of hours:minutes:seconds (e.g. 00:3:00 for 3 minutes) in the timer field above.
  2. You can leave the it blank (e.g 00:00:00) to an incremental counter timer.
  3. Click the "play" button to start the countdown.
  4. To pause the countdown, click the "pause" button. To resume, click "play" again.
  5. Keep in mind the incremental timer when paused and resumed will start a coutdown.
  6. To reset the countdown, click the "reset" button. This will reset the time to 00:00:00

Note: The timer will automatically stop when it reaches 0.
